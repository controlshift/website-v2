Distributed Events

  • Easily set up pages where your supporters can sign up to host and attend events.
  • Template content for related events like days of actions, with a central map and directory page.
  • Tools for hosts to manage their event and communicate with attendees.
  • Administrative features for your organization to track events and coach and manage hosts.

Local Organizing Teams

  • Help your members to find their closest groups or organize a new one if there are none nearby.
  • Mailing lists and member directories allow for local coordination.
  • Staff admin tools for coordinating your global network of teams.
  • Local groups grow through events they host and campaigns they create.
Learn More About Local Groups

Member Petitions

  • Give your supporters the tools to start launch their own campaign and recruit supporters.
  • The Efforts feature lets you set up groupings of similar campaigns with template content and a central map and directory page to recruit local leaders and supporters.
  • Campaign leaders get tools to message their supporters and recruit them to in-person events.
  • Administrative features let your organization moderate, approve, and manage campaigns and leaders.
